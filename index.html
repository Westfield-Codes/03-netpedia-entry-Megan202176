Effect of Social Media on Mental Health

Teenagers today spend an average of 4.8 hours per day on various social media platforms, such as Instagram, YouTube, and TikTok. While these platforms provide entertainment, connection, and information. Though they can also have serious negative side effects, especially for teenagers.

Too much social media use has been linked to higher rates of depression and anxiety for teenagers. Social media often has filtered images that can create unrealistic expectations. This can result in poor body image and low self-esteem.
